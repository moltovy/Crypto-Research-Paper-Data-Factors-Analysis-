Farside Investors — ETF flow exports (daily table only)

Source pages (as published by Farside):
- Bitcoin: https://farside.co.uk/bitcoin-etf-flow-all-data/
- Ethereum: https://farside.co.uk/ethereum-etf-flow-all-data/

Units: all flow figures are in millions of US dollars (US$m), matching the site title. Empty cells in the CSV correspond to “-” on the website (no flow / not applicable). Values shown in parentheses on the site (outflows) are stored as negative numbers in the CSV.

---

1) Bitcoin spot ETF daily flows

File: Farside ETF Data/BTC/bitcoin_etf_flow_all_data.csv

Description: One row per trading day with net flow (US$m) per ticker column plus a Total column. Dates are normalized to ISO format (YYYY-MM-DD) in UTC calendar terms (the site uses London-style day labels without explicit timezone).

Columns: Date, IBIT, FBTC, BITB, ARKB, BTCO, EZBC, BRRR, HODL, BTCW, MSBT, GBTC, BTC, Total

Row count: 579 data rows (daily observations; excludes Fee/Seed header rows from the page).

Date range: 2024-01-11 through 2026-04-10 (min/max Date in the CSV).

---

2) Ethereum spot ETF daily flows

File: Farside ETF Data/ETH/ethereum_etf_flow_all_data.csv

Description: Same convention as Bitcoin — daily net flows in US$m per ETF ticker and Total.

Columns: Date, ETHA, ETHB, FETH, ETHW, TETH, ETHV, QETH, EZET, ETHE, ETH, Total

Row count: 441 data rows.

Date range: 2024-07-23 through 2026-04-10 (min/max Date in the CSV).
